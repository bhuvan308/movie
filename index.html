PRABHAS

Prabhas, born Uppalapati Venkata Suryanarayana Prabhas Raju, is an Indian actor primarily known for his work in Telugu cinema and widely celebrated for his charismatic on-screen presence. He gained nationwide fame through his role in the epic film series Baahubali, directed by S.S. Rajamouli. Born on October 23, 1979, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, Prabhas comes from a family with ties to the film industry; his father, Uppalapati Suryanarayana Raju, was a producer, and his uncle, Krishnam Raju, was a veteran actor in the Telugu film industry. Prabhas began his career in Telugu cinema with the 2002 film Eeswar, but it was his role in the 2004 film Varsham that established him as a promising lead actor. His career continued to ascend as he starred in several successful films, including Chatrapathi (2005), directed by Rajamouli, which became a major hit. Known for his action-oriented roles, Prabhas quickly garnered a reputation for portraying strong, resilient characters, a quality that resonated with audiences and earned him a loyal fanbase.

ALLU ARJUN

Allu Arjun, born on April 8, 1983, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, is an immensely popular Indian actor primarily known for his work in Telugu cinema. He belongs to the illustrious Allu family, with his father Allu Aravind being a prominent film producer and his grandfather Allu Ramalingaiah a respected comedian and actor. Allu Arjun has made a significant mark in Indian cinema with his distinct style, remarkable dance skills, and unique performances, which have won him a large fanbase across South India and beyond. He began his acting journey as a child artist in the film Vijetha (1985) and later appeared in Daddy (2001). However, his major breakthrough came with his lead debut in Gangotri (2003), directed by K. Raghavendra Rao. Though his debut was well-received, it was his performance in Arya (2004) that established him as a rising star. Directed by Sukumar, Arya showcased his energetic performance and his dance prowess, earning him a Nandi Special Jury Award and cementing his place in the industry.
